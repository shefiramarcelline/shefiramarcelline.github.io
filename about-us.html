About us

Community of strong, curious, and ambitious women redefining success on our own terms.

Our
Mission


To advance women and girl’s social, economic and civics rights. We exist to redefine success for millennial women by providing the tools and connections they need to own their futures.

Our
Vision


Guided by the core values of integrity, accountability, respect and creativity, we envisions a world in which the most vulnerable people will have the power to lift themselves out of poverty and to create vital, healthy lives for their families and communities now and for the future.

Working At Eunoia

We believe our people are our most valuable assets. Our purpose and mission are manifested in our internal culture, with a focus on helping smart, ambitious people thrive in the workplace. This means that every manager at Eunoia knows that leading, cultivating the talent on their team, and helping each team member learn through both guidance and experimentation is their core priority.
